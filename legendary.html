Introduction

Legendary soups represent the pinnacle of culinary achievement and magical craftsmanship. These recipes call for ingredients harvested from legendary creatures, each one a monument to power incarnate. The recipes that follow are not for the faint of heart. They demand ingredients of incredible scarcity, hours or days of careful preparation, and consequences that reshape those who consume them.

A legendary soup follows these fundamental rules:

  • No thermos is needed to maintain a legendary soup's properties. Once prepared, its benefits manifest immediately and require no magical vessel.
  • Minimum cooking time is 8 hours. Some legendary recipes require significantly longer.
  • Legendary soups cannot be enhanced by Soup of the Day, Culinary Edge, or Divinely Delicious features.
  • Only one creature can cook a legendary soup. Multiple cooks attempting to prepare the same batch causes the recipe to spoil entirely.
  • The ingredient is consumed in the cooking process. It cannot be recovered, reused, or divided among multiple batches.
  • Harvesting an ingredient from a defeated legendary creature requires DM approval and must occur within a strict time window after the creature's death.

These soups are works of art, power made palatable. They are the dreams of heroes and the obsessions of those who dare to aspire to immortality through broth.

Legendary Recipes
Tarrasque Tail Consomme
Legendary Recipe
The marrow of the world-devourer, distilled to its essence. A soup that remakes the body in the image of destruction itself.
Ingredient
Tarrasque tail marrow
Cooking Time
8 hours
Serves
1 creature
Effects
  • Maximum hit points increased by 50
  • Resistance to nonmagical bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing damage
  • Regenerate 10 hit points at the start of your turn
  • Immunity to the frightened condition

These effects are permanent.

Beholder Eye Bisque
Legendary Recipe
Crafted from the beholder's central eye, harvested whole before the creature's essence fades. Vision perfected to an impossible degree.
Ingredient
Central eye removed whole within 10 minutes of death
Cooking Time
12 hours
Serves
1 creature
Effects (7 Days)
  • Truesight out to 120 feet
  • Immunity to gaze attacks
  • See invisible creatures and objects as if they were visible
  • Once per day, create a 30-foot cone of antimagic (as the antimagic field spell)

Effects fade after 7 days.

Lich Phylactery Potage
Legendary Recipe
The phylactery must be shattered over the broth at the moment of the lich's final death. Undeath itself becomes an ingredient, binding the drinker to eternity.
Ingredient
Phylactery shattered at lich's final death
Cooking Time
24 hours
Serves
1 creature
Effects (Permanent)
  • You no longer age
  • Advantage on Intelligence checks and saving throws
  • Once per long rest, when you would be reduced to 0 hit points, you are instead reduced to 1 hit point
  • Proficiency (or expertise if you already have it) with Arcana, History, and Religion checks

These effects are permanent.

Ancient Dragon Bone Broth
Legendary Recipe
The femur of an ancient wyrm, boiled under magical heat until nothing remains but essence and power. Draconic majesty made sustenance.
Ingredient
Femur of ancient dragon CR 20+
Cooking Time
10 hours with magical heat
Serves
4 creatures
Effects (30 Days)
  • Immunity to the damage type of the dragon's breath weapon
  • Strength and Constitution each increase by 4 (maximum 24)
  • Advantage on Intimidation checks and saving throws against the frightened condition
  • Once per day, you can exhale a 30-foot cone of the dragon's breath weapon type (DC 20 saving throw, same damage as the dragon's breath)

Effects fade after 30 days.

Elder Brain Bouillabaisse
Legendary Recipe
The elder brain must be harvested whole within thirty minutes of the hive's destruction. As you cook, the lingering psychic energy assaults your mind with visions of collective thought.
Ingredient
Elder brain harvested whole within 30 minutes
Cooking Time
8 hours (3 DC 16 Wisdom saves per hour or take 4d6 psychic damage)
Serves
6 creatures
Effects (10 Days)
  • Telepathy out to 120 feet with creatures you can see
  • Cast detect thoughts at will (no spell slot required)
  • Once per day, cast dominate monster (DC 18 Intelligence saving throw to resist)
  • Immunity to charm and frighten effects caused by aberrations

Effects fade after 10 days.

Kraken Ink Ramen
Legendary Recipe
One part ink to one hundred parts seawater, simmered near the shore where the beast was slain. The depths themselves settle into every bowl.
Ingredient
Ink sac diluted 1:100 in seawater
Cooking Time
8 hours near water
Serves
4 creatures
Effects (14 Days)
  • Swim speed of 60 feet and ability to breathe underwater
  • Immunity to lightning damage
  • Once per day, exhale a cloud of ink in a 20-foot radius (creatures in the area must make a DC 18 Constitution saving throw or be blinded for 1 minute)
  • Ability to communicate telepathically with creatures that can swim

Effects fade after 14 days.

Tiamat's Five-Spice Hotpot
Legendary Recipe
One scale harvested from each of Tiamat's five draconic heads. An recipe that has never been successfully completed. Legend says the cooking time multiplies by the cook's doubt.
Ingredient
One scale from each head of Tiamat
Cooking Time
5 hours (1 per scale at increasing difficulty)
Serves
1 creature
Effects (Permanent)
  • Immunity to fire, cold, lightning, acid, and poison damage
  • Once per day, exhale a 30-foot cone of elemental damage (fire, cold, lightning, acid, or poison; DC 20 Dexterity saving throw, 10d8 damage)
  • Advantage on Charisma checks and saving throws when dealing with chromatic dragons

This recipe has never been successfully completed in known history.

Aboleth Memory Bisque
Legendary Recipe
The mucus glands must be rendered immediately, before they break down. The cooking requires absolute attention. One moment of wavering focus and the entire batch curdles into uselessness.
Ingredient
Mucus glands rendered immediately (break down in 2 hours)
Cooking Time
16 hours on low heat (DC 14 Wisdom per hour or lost in visions; burns after 20 hours)
Serves
1 creature
Effects (Permanent)
  • Proficiency with all skills (you gain proficiency bonus on any ability check you make)
  • Cast legend lore at will (no spell slot required)
  • Advantage on all Intelligence saving throws and Intelligence checks
  • Once per week, spend 1 hour in meditation to receive a vision of a past event within 10,000 years

These effects are permanent.

Vecna's Unseen Eye Stew
Legendary Recipe
The necrotic residue from the socket of one who once bore the Eye of Vecna. Must be prepared in total darkness. Even candlelight will spoil it, turning the broth into useless sludge.
Ingredient
Necrotic residue from Eye of Vecna bearer's socket
Cooking Time
6 hours in total darkness (light within 30 feet curdles it)
Serves
1 creature
Effects (Permanent)
  • See invisible creatures and objects within 60 feet, even through magical darkness
  • Once per day, cast foresight on yourself (no spell slot required)
  • Always know if a creature within 30 feet of you is lying

Drawback: You have disadvantage on saving throws against effects caused by Vecna.

Dracolich Marrow Glaze
Legendary Recipe
Not a soup in the traditional sense. A glaze of crystallized marrow vapor, captured in a sealed vessel and applied to other soups. Death itself, rendered into enhancement.
Ingredient
Crystallized marrow vapor in sealed vessel
Cooking Time
4 hours, applied to another non-legendary recipe
Serves
Applied to existing soup
Effects When Applied (7 Days)
  • Any healing received also grants temporary hit points equal to the amount healed (last for 24 hours)
  • Resistance to necrotic damage for 7 days
  • No need to sleep and immunity to exhaustion for 7 days
  • Once during those 7 days, when you would be reduced to 0 hit points, you instead drop to 1 hit point and are immune to all damage until the end of your next turn

Effects fade after 7 days.
