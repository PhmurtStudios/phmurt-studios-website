The Studio
About Phmurt Studios
Original tabletop design, strange ideas, and player-first tools

Phmurt Studios is a fantasy-forward design space focused on original D&D classes, homebrew systems, interactive character tools, and resources that help tables play faster, stranger, and with more personality.

What this studio is building
Tools that help a table get to the fun faster

The goal of the site is simple: make it easier to discover weird classes, create memorable characters, manage homebrew, and actually use that content at the table.

That means blending public-facing content like the Grimoire with more practical systems like the Character Builder, printable sheets, saved character storage, and a Compendium that can grow into a real workshop for custom content.

Roll well. Play weird.
A studio identity built around fantasy atmosphere, handcrafted homebrew, and tools that feel like part of the world instead of separate from it.
Core Pillars
Homebrew With Identity
Classes, subclasses, races, backgrounds, items, and original systems should feel authored, flavorful, and playable — not just mechanically complete.
Practical Table Tools
Builders, saved characters, printable sheets, and compendium workflows should be genuinely useful during prep and at the table — not just nice to look at.
Atmosphere Matters
The site should feel like a world, not a spreadsheet. Typography, iconography, color, and language all support that fantasy-studio identity.
