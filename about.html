Philosophy

Good game design is invisible. The mechanics should tell a story before a word of flavor is read. A class feature should feel like a character choice, not a stat boost. When a player reads a Phmurt Studios class, they should immediately know who this person is — not just what they can do.

Every piece of content we release is playtested, iterated, and refined until it feels right. Not just mechanically balanced, but narratively coherent. The Soup Savant is not a gimmick class. It is a fully realized character concept that happens to involve soup — and that distinction matters.

Currently Available

Our first release is the Soup Savant — a full 20-level D&D 5e class — along with the Legendary Soups supplement. More content is in development.
