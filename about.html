About Phmurt Studios
Independent Game Design

We are a small independent studio dedicated to creating high-quality tabletop RPG content. From custom D&D classes to original game systems, every product is playtested, refined, and built for storytellers.

What We Do

Phmurt Studios is an independent game design studio focused on creating premium tabletop RPG content. We specialize in D&D 5e homebrew classes, original TTRPG systems, and digital tools that empower storytellers and players. Every product we release has been rigorously playtested and refined by our community.

We believe in playtested over polished, content that works at your table, not just looks good on paper. Our philosophy is simple: make games that inspire imagination and bring people together around the table.

◆

Our Philosophy

Great game design is built on feedback. We playtest extensively, listen to our community, and iterate until it feels right. We design for storytellers first, mechanics second. Whether you're running a campaign or exploring a new system, our content is crafted to enhance your table and inspire your next adventure.

D&D Homebrew
Custom classes and supplements for D&D 5e. Fully playable, extensively tested, and designed to fit seamlessly into your campaign.
Original Systems
Standalone RPG systems built from scratch. Complete rulesets for new genres and play styles, ready to drop into your table.
Digital Tools
Character builders, fillable sheets, and browser-based compendiums. No software to install, no paywalls, just pure utility for your table.
Community
Join us on Discord, watch live games on StartPlaying, and explore video content on YouTube. Connect with other creators and players.
